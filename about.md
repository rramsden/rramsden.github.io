# About

I'm Richard Ramsden. In 2013 I left Canada for a new life in Japan, and I've been
building software and things ever since.

This is where I write — about software, building things, leadership, and the
occasional detour into hardware. The name, *Keep It Simple Stupid*, is both a
principle I try to work by and a reminder to myself when I overcomplicate things.
